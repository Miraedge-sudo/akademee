About us

A legacy of academic excellence in Cameroon

Founded in 1998, Grace Bilingual Academy has grown to become one of Douala's most respected institutions. We combine rigorous academic standards with a nurturing environment where every student is empowered to reach their full potential.

Our dedicated team of 32 qualified teachers delivers the Cameroonian anglophone curriculum from Form 1 through Upper Sixth, preparing students for GCE Ordinary and Advanced Level examinations.
