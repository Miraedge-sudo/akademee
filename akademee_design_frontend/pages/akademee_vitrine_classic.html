Our heritage

A legacy built on
discipline & vision

Founded in 1998, Grace Bilingual Academy has grown from a small institution of 40 students to one of Douala's most respected secondary schools. For over two decades, we have upheld a singular commitment: delivering world-class education through bilingual instruction, rigorous academics, and the development of character.

"We do not simply teach subjects — we shape citizens who will lead Cameroon and the world."

— The Principal, Grace Bilingual Academy

Our 32 qualified educators bring expertise across sciences, arts, and commercial streams, guiding students through GCE Ordinary and Advanced Level examinations with a consistent 94% pass rate.
